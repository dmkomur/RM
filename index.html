    Rick Sanchez

    Rick Sanchez is a brilliant but deeply troubled scientist and one of the main characters in the animated TV show Rick and Morty. He often acts selfishly and impulsively, using his scientific knowledge and gadgets to manipulate those around him.

    Morty Smith

    Morty Smith is the other main character in the animated TV show Rick and Morty, and Rick's grandson. He is a timid and anxious teenager who is often reluctant to accompany Rick on his wild adventures, but is forced to do so anyway. Despite his fears, Morty is often able to show surprising courage and resourcefulness, and is a loyal and loving grandson to Rick.

    Summer Smith

    Summer Smith is Morty's older sister and one of the main characters in Rick and Morty. Initially portrayed as a typical teenager, she develops into a more complex and independent character, who is intelligent, resourceful, and fiercely loyal to her family.
